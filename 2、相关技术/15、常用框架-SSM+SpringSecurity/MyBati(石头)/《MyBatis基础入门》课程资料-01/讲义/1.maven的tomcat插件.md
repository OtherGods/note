# 1.maven的tomcat插件

## 1.1.配置tomcat插件

```xml
  <build>    
<!--tomcat的插件-->
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.apache.tomcat.maven</groupId>
        <artifactId>tomcat7-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>2.2</version>
        <configuration>
          <path>/</path>
          <port>8080</port>
        </configuration>
      </plugin>
    </plugins>
  </build>
```
